March 1984

Almost 2 years before the release of the NES, the Nintendo Vs. System is released in North American Arcades.

1984-1990

Over the next 6 years, Nintendo and third-party publishers release some of the biggest frachises of the day for the Nintendo Vs. System: Super Mario Bros, Castlevania, Excitebike, Gradius, Dr. Mario and many more!

1991+

And then... silence.

For 30 years. Nothing. Not a single new game is released for the Nintendo Vs. System. No commercial titles. No homebrew games. Nothing.

Until Now

Introducing

A brand new game for the Vs. Nintendo System. The first of it's kind in 30 years!

From Below is a brand new game released for the NES in November 2020, and ported to the Vs. Arcade system shortly after. It has gone through extensive updates to make this a true Vs. System game, and not just a quick port.

Featuring:

  • Coin-Op
  • Leaderboards
  • Redesigned Menus and Title Screen
  • All Settings Configurable via Dipswitches
  • Attract Mode
  • Free Play
  • Maintenance Button and Coin Counter Supported
  • Support most PPUs (RP2C04-0001/2/3/4) with 2C03/2C05 coming soon.
  • Works on both Vs. Unisystem and Dualsystem (both sides)
  • Original Instruction Card (with printing instructions)
